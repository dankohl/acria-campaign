Continental Divide

Acria is a collection of small, mostly landlocked, provinces. The continent is bordered by the Vermillion Sea to the east, and the Eclar mountains to the west. The mountain range spans from the southern edge of the Eclar Hinterlands (it's southern most province) to the western ridge of Durnfaldir, City of Dwarves. As the range continues north, it tapers off into a dense Tundra forest of Leirafell.

Inner-continental confilct has ravaged the provinces for the better part of the millenia. Since the human Odrian Empire fractured into it's four primary provinces, Solime, Eshela, Eclar, and the modern-day Odria, humanity has been dominated by quarrels for dominance.

The power vaccum left by the downfall of the Odrian Empire has lent much power to the nations of Leirafell and Durnfaldir. The Elves of Leirafell have used their superior control of the arcane to "loan" out their citizens as powerful mercenaries. Durnfaldir, however, opts to serve as the economic trade center. The kingdom of Dwarves often bankrolls military conquests of the splintered human empires.

The heroes of our story find themselves in a small human settlement in the northernmost, hospitable part of Leirafell. Exiled from their small village, these heroes find that not all in Hanatun is at is seems.
